About Flowmingo Studios

We're an independent software studio dedicated to creating thoughtful, user-focused applications.

Our Mission

Flowmingo Studios is an independent software studio focused on building simple, thoughtful consumer applications with a strong emphasis on design, usability, and real-world utility. We believe technology should enhance daily life in meaningful, accessible ways.

What We Do

We develop mobile applications that solve real problems for real people. Our apps combine cutting-edge AI technology with intuitive design to deliver experiences that are both powerful and easy to use. From hair care analysis to skincare insights, we're building tools that help people make better decisions about their personal care routines.

Our Approach

Every app we create starts with understanding the user's needs. We prioritize:

  • Design Excellence: Clean, intuitive interfaces that feel natural to use
  • Privacy First: Your data is yours. We handle it responsibly and transparently
  • Real-World Value: Every feature serves a genuine purpose in your daily life
  • Continuous Improvement: We listen to our users and evolve our products based on feedback

Our Commitment

We're committed to providing exceptional support for all our products. If you have questions, feedback, or need assistance, our team is here to help. We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days.

Have questions about our studio or want to learn more? We'd love to hear from you. Contact us and we'll be happy to connect.
