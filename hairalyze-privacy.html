Hairalyze Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Hairalyze ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the rights you have.

By using Hairalyze, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Who We Are

Hairalyze provides AI-powered hair analysis and feedback based on photos you upload. We use third-party AI providers, including OpenAI, to process images and generate responses, but we do not use your data to train or improve AI models.

If you have questions or data-related requests, email us at hairalyze@gmail.com.

3. Age Requirements

Hairalyze is intended for individuals 13 years of age or older in the U.S. and most countries. In the European Union, users must be 16 years or older, or the minimum digital-consent age in their country.

We do not knowingly collect or store personal data from anyone under these ages. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us data, contact hairalyze@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

4. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use Hairalyze:

a. User-Provided Data

  • Uploaded photos or images for analysis
  • Hair goals or descriptions you enter
  • Optional account details such as email or username

b. Automatically Collected Data

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage statistics
  • Anonymous analytics such as crashes and performance
  • IP address for security and localization

We do not collect or process biometric identifiers beyond what is necessary to analyze hair in your submitted photo.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use your data only to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Display analysis and scan history
  • Improve stability, usability, and performance
  • Respond to support inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not train AI models on your images, sell or rent your data to third parties, or use your content for advertising or marketing without consent.

6. How Your Data Is Stored

Your images and related data are stored securely on encrypted servers, including services such as Supabase. Access is restricted to authorized staff who may view data only for technical maintenance, debugging, or support.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to operate Hairalyze and fulfill your requests. When you delete a scan or request deletion, your content is permanently removed within a reasonable period.

We may retain anonymized logs, without identifying information, for analytics and security.

8. Data Deletion and Access Requests

You can delete scans directly in the app or request account or data deletion by emailing hairalyze@gmail.com. We will confirm and process all deletion or data-access requests in accordance with applicable laws.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed or stored in the United States or other countries where we or our service providers operate. We use appropriate safeguards, such as standard contractual clauses, to protect your data when transferred internationally.

10. Legal Basis for Processing (EU Users)

For users in the European Economic Area, we process your personal data under one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Consent: You provide explicit consent when uploading photos
  • Contract: Processing is necessary to deliver the Service you request
  • Legitimate Interests: For app performance, security, and troubleshooting
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable law

11. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to certain data processing
  • Request a copy of your data for portability

To exercise these rights, contact hairalyze@gmail.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by law.

12. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party providers to operate the Service, including OpenAI for processing and Supabase for storage. These providers are bound by confidentiality and data-protection agreements and may only process your data on our behalf.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services outside our control.

13. Cookies and Analytics

We may use limited cookies or similar technologies to improve functionality and measure performance. You can disable cookies in your browser or device settings, though some features may not function properly.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make material changes, we will update the Effective Date and, if required, notify users through the app or by other reasonable means. Continued use of Hairalyze after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

15. Contact Us

For any questions, feedback, or privacy-related requests, contact hairalyze@gmail.com.

By using Hairalyze, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
