Hairalyze Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Hairalyze (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how it is processed, and your rights.

By using Hairalyze, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Nature of the Service

Hairalyze provides AI-generated cosmetic haircare insights based on photos that you voluntarily upload. The Service is designed for informational and grooming purposes only and is not a medical or diagnostic tool.

3. Age Requirements

The Service is intended for users aged 13 or older. In regions where the digital age of consent is higher (such as parts of the European Union), users must meet the applicable minimum age or use the Service only with parental or guardian consent.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children below the applicable minimum age. If you believe a child has provided data to us, contact hairalyze@gmail.com and we will delete it.

4. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Photos uploaded for hair analysis
  • Hair goals, preferences, or optional descriptions
  • Account identifiers such as Apple Sign-In or email (if provided)

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage analytics and performance diagnostics
  • IP address and approximate location for security purposes
  • Marketing attribution identifiers (such as AppsFlyer install data)

Hairalyze does not use facial recognition or collect biometric identifiers for identity verification purposes.

5. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide AI-generated analysis and maintain scan history
  • Operate, improve, and troubleshoot the Service
  • Measure app performance, installs, and marketing effectiveness
  • Respond to support or legal requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your photos or personal data.

We do not use your photos to train our own AI models.

6. AI Processing and Third-Party Providers

To generate analysis results, uploaded images are securely stored on infrastructure providers such as Supabase. Images may be temporarily accessed by trusted AI processing providers (such as OpenAI) using secure, time-limited access methods.

These providers process images solely to generate requested analysis and are contractually restricted from using your data for unrelated purposes.

7. Analytics and Attribution

We use analytics and attribution tools (such as AppsFlyer) to understand how users discover and interact with the Service. These tools may collect device identifiers, install data, and usage information in accordance with platform permissions and applicable laws.

8. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure using authentication controls, encryption, and access restrictions. While we implement reasonable safeguards, no system can guarantee absolute security.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations. When you delete scans or request account deletion, data is permanently removed within a reasonable timeframe, except where limited retention is required for security or legal compliance.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. We use appropriate safeguards to protect personal data transferred internationally.

11. Legal Basis for Processing (EEA Users)

For users in the European Economic Area, processing is based on one or more of the following:

  • Your consent when uploading photos
  • Performance of a contract to provide requested features
  • Legitimate interests in improving security and reliability
  • Compliance with legal obligations

12. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Object to certain processing
  • Request data portability

Requests can be submitted to hairalyze@gmail.com.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material updates will be reflected by a revised Effective Date and communicated where required.

14. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact hairalyze@gmail.com.
