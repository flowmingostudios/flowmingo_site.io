Troubleshooting Guide

App Won't Launch

  • Make sure your device is running the latest iOS version
  • Try force-closing the app and reopening it
  • Restart your device
  • Check if an app update is available in the App Store

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Close the app completely and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Check for app updates
  • Free up storage space on your device
  • If the issue persists, contact us with details about when the crash occurs

Features Not Working

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Check that you've granted necessary permissions (Camera, Photos, etc.)
  • Update to the latest version of the app
  • Review the app's settings to ensure features are enabled

Data or Settings Issues

  • Check your device's storage space
  • Ensure you're signed in if the app requires an account
  • Try logging out and back in
  • If data is missing, check if you're using the correct account
