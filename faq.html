General Questions

What is Flowmingo Studios?

Flowmingo Studios is an independent software studio focused on building simple, thoughtful consumer applications with a strong emphasis on design, usability, and real-world utility.

What apps do you develop?

We currently have Hairalyze available, with Poreyo coming soon. Our apps focus on providing practical, AI-powered solutions for everyday needs.

How can I stay updated on new apps?

Follow us on the App Store or check back on our website for announcements about new releases and updates.

Hairalyze

What is Hairalyze?

Hairalyze is an AI-powered app that provides personalized hair analysis and care insights to help you achieve your best hair health.

How does the AI analysis work?

Hairalyze uses advanced AI technology to analyze your hair characteristics and provide personalized recommendations based on your unique hair type and needs.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All data is processed securely and we never share your personal information with third parties.

How do I report a bug or issue?

Please use our contact form and select "App Support / Bug Report" as the subject. Include as much detail as possible about the issue you're experiencing.

Support & Contact

How can I contact support?

You can reach us through our contact form or email us directly at flowmingostudios@gmail.com. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.

Can I request a feature?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Use our contact form and select "Feature Request" as the subject. We review all suggestions and consider them for future updates.

What should I do if my app crashes?

If you experience crashes or technical issues, please report them through our support page or contact form. Include details about when the crash occurred and what you were doing at the time.
